COMPANY PROFILE

HD Digital Signs Corporation is a Filipino-owned out-of-home (OOH) advertising company specializing in LED boards, static billboards, lamp post banners, and other outdoor media. The company was formed by industry leaders with over 30 years of experience, collaborating to bring innovation and high-definition impact to outdoor advertising.
